Messege From Honorable Vice Chancellor

As a part of Quality Enhancement in Higher Education in Bangladesh, the University Grants Commission of Bangladesh (UGC) under the World Bank credit scheme has taken a program on Higher Education Quality Enhancement Project (HEQEP) to strengthen the overall capacity building in the tertiary level for quality education through the establishment and implementation of Institutional Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) at each of the Institutions (both private and public) in the country.

Messege From Honorable Director of IQAC

As a part of Quality Enhancement in Higher Education in Bangladesh, the University Grants Commission of Bangladesh (UGC) under the World Bank credit scheme has taken a program on Higher Education Quality Enhancement Project (HEQEP) to strengthen the overall capacity building in the tertiary level for quality education through the establishment and implementation of Institutional Quality Assurance Cell (IQAC) at each of the Institutions (both private and public) in the country.

QAC

The IQAC shall report to the VC and be supervised by the University's QAC. The composition of the QAC shall be as follows:

  1. Hon'ble Vice-Chancellor, BUET– Chairman
  2. Dean, Faculty of Architecture and Planning, BUET – Chairman
  3. Dean, Faculty of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, BUET– Member
  4. Dean, Faculty of Civil Engineering, BUET– Member
  5. Hon'ble Vice-Chancellor, BUET– Member
  6. Hon'ble Vice-Chancellor, BUET– Member

Meet The Team

OBJECTIVES

  • Institutionalize the quality assurance culture in accordance with national QA guidelines and international practices;
  • Ensure that the university’s quality assurance procedures are designed following the QAU guidelines and national requirements;
  • Develop, maintain and enhance quality of education and people’s perception in favour of the university through consistent quality assurance practice and performance;
  • Build image of the university with confidence of the stakeholders ensuring transparency, accountability, and good practices in all aspects of management; and
  • Prepare the university to meet the external quality assurance assessment and accreditation requirements.

Functions of IQAC

  • Institutionalize the quality assurance culture in accordance with national QA guidelines and international practices;
